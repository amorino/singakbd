## Changelog
### R1 (2021)
#### Features
- Leaf spring Aluminum or Normal Polycarbonate plate
- Poron gasket strips
- Hand-polished CNC Milled 6063 Aluminum
- Sandblasted coated Copper weight
- Average build weighs: approx 2000g
- 1.5 mm plates in Black Aluminum and Polycarbonate
- Acrylic slide in back cover
- Hiney PCB
#### Colors
- Red
- Gray
- Silver

### R2 (2023)
#### Features
- Kit Plate: Aluminum Leaf Spring with Addon Plates: CF Leaf spring / PC Plate available to purchase
- Poron gasket strips
- Hand-polished CNC Milled 6063 Aluminum
- Sandblasted Brass or Stainless steel weight
- PCB Solder and Hotswap available
- Tempered glass back cover
- PCB by Wolf
#### Changes from previous versions
- Weight is now three-piece.
#### Colors
- Autumn Lilac (Stainless steel weight)
- British Racing Green (Sandblasted brass weight)
- Smoke Grey (Stainless steel weight)
- Lunar Black (Sandblasted brass weight)
